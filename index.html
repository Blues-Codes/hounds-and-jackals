To Start: Click the "Who goes first?" button above to flip the Silver Coin and see who goes first!

How to Play:
Click the three Golden Coins above to move your game piece!
    How many spaces do you move?
  • 1 head and move 1 space
  • 2 heads and move 2 spaces
  • 3 heads and move 3 spaces
  • 3 tails and move 5 spaces

Be careful of the Cobras!
Land on any of the Cobra tails and immediately you'll be moved to the cobra head!
Land on any of the Cobra heads and immediately you'll be moved to the Cobra tail!

How to win:
Be the first to move your piece to the Golden star at the end of the game!
