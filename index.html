To Start: Be the first to get Heads on the Silver coin to go first!

How to Play:
Flip the three Golden Coins to see how many spaces you move! • 1 head and move 1 space • 2 heads and move 2 spaces • 3 heads and move 3 spaces • 3 tails and move 5 spaces plus get an extra turn!
Be careful of the Cobras!
Land on any of the Cobra tails and immediately you'll be moved to the cobra head!
Land on any of the Cobra heads and immediately you'll be moved to the Cobra tail!
Land on space 15 or 25 and take another turn!
At the start of every turn, if your piece is not on the start space, you can flip the Silver Coin to get another piece onto the board. Flip heads and you've added a new piece to the board. Flip tails and you can not add a new piece to the board.

How to win:
Be the first to move all of your pieces to the Golden star at the end of the game!
